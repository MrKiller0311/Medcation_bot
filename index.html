Welcome to SymptoCheck

SymptoCheck is a smart healthcare website that helps users analyze symptoms, connect with certified doctors, locate nearby clinics, and access health resources. Designed to bridge the gap between self-diagnosis and professional medical consultation.

AI Symptom Checker

Get instant analysis of your symptoms with AI-powered predictions.

Telemedicine Support

Consult certified doctors online from anywhere, anytime.

Clinic Locator

Find nearby hospitals and clinics using Google Maps integration.

Emergency Alerts

Trigger real-time alerts to hospitals in case of critical conditions.
